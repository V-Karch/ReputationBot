**These are on a per Pokémon basis**. Most mythicals are not shiny huntable, and the method of hunting varies quite a bit between the ones that are. Be sure to research these yourself because they can change and you don't want to get it wrong with these.
## Mythicals
- **Arceus** BDSP: 1:(40-60+)
Shiny huntable ONLY in BDSP. Completed PLA save file is needed and only catchable in post game. Arceus can be a 20+ hour long hunt, so it would take a lot to trade for a legit one.
There was an event for shiny Arceus, but it was based on luck, only in Japan, and came with a purchased movie ticket, so you'll never find someone with a legit one. cloned/genned ones you could probably get for ~5 or so shinies.

- **Jirachi** N/A
Just get an event one. it will be cloned, but just trade for a genned/cloned shiny Jirachi from an event for ~5 shinies.
If you're set on a legit one, there are 2 ways to hunt it. 1: Pokémon Colosseum bonus disk (requires ~$500 of hardware/game disks just to be able to encounter the dang thing), 2: PoGo Stamped. (really long quests, paid research. better measured in USD)

- **Manaphy**: 1:15
Pokémon HOME BDSP Pokédex completion reward, hard to prove legitamacy.
Also huntable with pokémon Ranger Manaphy egg, but 1 per cartridge, so very very rare. 

- **Phione**: 1:5
Easily huntable via Masuda method with Ditto and Manaphy.

- **Celebi**: IDK
Shiny huntable in Pokémon Crystal.

- **Shaymin**: 1:(20-25)
Shiny huntable in Diamond and Pearl via void glitch, simple reset hunt, and in BDSP via surf glitch.

- **Darkrai**: 1:(20-25)
Shiny huntable in Diamond and Pearl via void glitch, simple reset hunt, and in BDSP via surf glitch.

- **Mew**: 1:(25-30)
Shiny huntable in Emerald with Pomeg glitch.

- **Deoxys**: 1:(25-30)
Sometimes in POGO raids, with POGO stamp/custom OT being worth more.

- **Genesect**: 1:(25-30)
Sometimes in POGO raids, with POGO stamp/custom OT being worth more.

- **Keldeo/Meltan/Enamorous/Meloetta**: 1-15
Only obtainable as Pokédex completion rewards.

- **Zeraora** 1:15
Hard to prove legitamacy, event only.
HOME STAMP is worth hundreds in USD, so won't put a shiny price on it lol.
