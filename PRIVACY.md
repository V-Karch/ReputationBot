# Chandelure Discord Bot Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 11, 2026

## 1. Overview

Chandelure is a Discord bot designed to help Pokémon trading communities establish trust between members through a reputation system. This Privacy Policy explains what information is stored, why it is stored, and how it is used.

By using Chandelure, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

Chandelure stores only the information necessary to operate its reputation system.

This may include:

* Discord User IDs of users receiving reputation
* Discord User IDs of users submitting reputation
* Positive or negative reputation values
* Reputation reasons and reviews submitted by users
* Internal entry identifiers required to manage reputation records

Chandelure does **not** collect or store:

* Discord message content
* Message history or message logs
* Direct messages
* Uploaded files or attachments
* Email addresses
* IP addresses
* Browsing activity
* Information unrelated to the reputation system

## 3. How Information Is Used

Stored information is used exclusively to:

* Record reputation feedback between users
* Display reputation scores and reputation history
* Allow moderators to manage reputation entries
* Promote trust and accountability within Pokémon trading communities

Information is never used for advertising, marketing, analytics, or profiling purposes.

## 4. Data Retention

Reputation records are retained as long as they remain relevant to the operation of the reputation system.

Because reputation history is a core feature of Chandelure, records may be retained indefinitely unless removed by moderation actions, administrative actions, or maintenance procedures.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Chandelure does not sell, rent, trade, or share stored information with third parties.

Information may only be disclosed:

* When required by applicable law
* In response to a valid legal request
* To protect the security, integrity, or operation of the bot

## 6. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored reputation information from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or deletion.

No method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 7. User Rights

Users may request the removal of reputation information where permitted by server administrators or the bot operator.

Because reputation records are intended to provide historical trust information, removal requests may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

## 8. Third-Party Services

Chandelure operates through Discord and relies on Discord's platform to function.

Use of Discord is subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in functionality, legal requirements, or operational practices.

Continued use of Chandelure after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
